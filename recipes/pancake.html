Pancakes

Who doesn't love to have pancakes for a cozy weekened breakfast. These pancakes will be a huge hit for the family and for the cook; they are easy to make and aboslutely delicious to eat! They are fluffy and soft with just the right amount of sweetness for a weekend treat. We top our pancakes off with a combination of jam, nutella, peanutbutter, butter, and, of course, homemande whipped cream!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
